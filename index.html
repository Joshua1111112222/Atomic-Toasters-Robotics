Welcome to the Atomic Toasters Robotics Team!

We are here to have fun, spread the word of God, learn, and do well in competitions. Our team has achieved notable success, including making it to states.

About Us

The Atomic Toasters Robotics Team is dedicated to promoting STEM education and fostering a passion for robotics among students. We participate in various competitions, such as FIRST Robotics. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators through hands-on learning and teamwork.
